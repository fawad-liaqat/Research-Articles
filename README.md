# Research Articles

Welcome to my research repository. These articles explore critical intersections of economics, technology, and human behavior—areas where rigorous analysis can illuminate complex policy and business challenges.

## Research Focus

My work aims to help people understand better. The articles here distill research and analysis into accessible insights on topics that matter: how tariffs create cascading economic effects, why last-mile adoption stumbles despite technological readiness, how digital currencies can serve populations beyond the traditional banking system, what compliance gaps exist in agentic AI systems, and how tokenized systems might reshape remittance flows.

Each article combines empirical research, real-world context, and practical implications. The goal is clarity—cutting through noise to surface what actually matters.

## Articles

- **[Tariff Cascades](articles/tariff-cascades/)** — How tariffs propagate through supply chains, amplifying economic friction beyond initial tariff rates.

- **[Behavioral Last-Mile](articles/behavioral-last-mile/)** — Why adoption failures often stem from behavioral friction rather than technology gaps, and what that means for rollout strategy.

- **[CBDC and Low Literacy](articles/cbdc-low-literacy/)** — Designing digital currency systems for populations with limited financial literacy or digital access.

- **[Agentic AI Compliance](articles/agentic-ai-compliance/)** — Regulatory and operational challenges in deploying autonomous AI systems at scale.

- **[Tokenized Remittances](articles/tokenized-remittances/)** — How tokenization could reduce friction and cost in cross-border money transfer for diaspora communities.
